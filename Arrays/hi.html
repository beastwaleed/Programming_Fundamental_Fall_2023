Exploring Artifical Intelligence

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence(AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machine that are programmed to think and learn like human.

Key components of AI
  • machine learning
  • Natural language processing
  • computer vision
  • robatics

Applications of AI

  • virtual assistants (e.g. Siri, Alexa)
  • Recomendation System
  • automation vehicles
  • healthcare dignostics

Challenges and Ethical Consideration

While AI offers many benefits, there are challenges such as bias algorithms, job displacement, and ethical concers regarding privacy and decision-making.
